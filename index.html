Everything you need

Built for how families
actually manage money

Not a banking app. Not a spreadsheet. Grip is the middle ground — simple enough to actually use, powerful enough to make a difference.

💰
Income Tracking
Log salaries, freelance income, side hustles — all sources in one place. Know exactly what's coming in each month.
📊
Expense Logging
Record every peso spent with categories, accounts, and dates. Edit or delete entries anytime — no locked history.
🎯
Budget Goals
Set monthly limits per category and watch color-coded progress bars tell you where you stand at a glance.
📋
Payables Tracker
Never miss a bill. Schedule recurring payables, mark them paid (with account tracking), skip months when needed.
🏦
Account Management
Track cash, bank accounts, credit cards, e-wallets, and investments — all with live balance updates.
👨‍👩‍👧
Household Sharing
Invite your partner or family members. Everyone sees the same picture. One household, one financial truth.
